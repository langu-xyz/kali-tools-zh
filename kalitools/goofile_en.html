Goofile 1.5

usage: goofile options

       -d: domain to search

       -f: filetype (ex. pdf)

example:./goofile.py -d test.com -f txt

goofile Usage Example

Search for files from a domain (-d kali.org) of the PDF filetype (-f pdf):

[email protected]:~# goofile -d kali.org -f pdf
